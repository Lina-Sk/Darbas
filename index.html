Our Artists

At ART STUDIO, we believe that art is more than just a visual experience—it's a reflection of creativity, emotion, and the human spirit. Our platform is dedicated to celebrating artists from all backgrounds by sharing their unique works with a global audience. Meet our Artists and explore their works!

Sofia DeLuna

Style:
Abstract Expressionism.
Background:
Sofia DeLuna, a self-taught artist from Buenos Aires, Argentina, began her journey with art at a young age. Her bold use of color and texture is a reflection of her passion for expressing emotions without boundaries. Influenced by the vibrant culture of her hometown, Sofia's work often features swirling shapes and dramatic contrasts that convey movement and depth. She believes in the power of art to communicate what words cannot, and her pieces invite viewers to explore their own interpretations.
Inspiration:
Sofia finds inspiration in the chaos and beauty of city life, the rhythms of nature, and the ever-changing landscape of human emotions. Her favorite medium is acrylic on canvas, which she manipulates to create multi-layered compositions that feel both chaotic and harmonious.

Liam Hargrove

Style:
Realism & Portraiture.
Background:
Originally from Edinburgh, Scotland, Liam Hargrove has spent the last decade perfecting his skills in realistic portraiture. With a background in fine arts, Liam's detailed brushwork captures the subtleties of human expressions, making each of his portraits a story in itself. His ability to bring out the personality of his subjects through his work has earned him recognition in numerous art exhibitions across Europe.
Inspiration:
Liam's primary inspiration comes from the everyday faces and stories of people he encounters. His paintings are a tribute to the hidden emotions and quiet moments that make each individual unique. He predominantly works with oil paints, allowing him to create lifelike textures and a sense of depth that draws viewers in.

Aria Chen

Style:
Digital Art & Fantasy Illustration.
Background:
Aria Chen is a digital artist based in San Francisco, California. With a degree in graphic design and animation, Aria's art blurs the line between reality and fantasy. Her digital illustrations often depict surreal landscapes, mythical creatures, and dreamlike scenes that transport viewers to another world. Aria's work has been featured in several gaming and animation projects, where her imagination takes center stage.
Inspiration:
Aria's artistic vision is fueled by her love of science fiction, folklore, and fantasy literature. She draws inspiration from nature, astronomy, and her fascination with storytelling. Working primarily with digital tools, Aria blends her technical skills with her creative spirit to produce mesmerizing artworks that captivate the imagination.

Marco Santorini

Style:
Contemporary Sculpture.
Background:
Marco Santorini, originally from Florence, Italy, is a sculptor who specializes in contemporary and conceptual pieces. His work is characterized by bold, geometric shapes and the innovative use of materials like metal, wood, and recycled objects. Marco's sculptures explore themes of human connection, transformation, and the relationship between nature and technology. His pieces have been featured in several public art installations and galleries across Europe.
Inspiration:
Marco's inspiration comes from modern architecture, urban landscapes, and the fusion of organic and industrial elements. He believes that every object has the potential to become a work of art and that even the most ordinary materials can be transformed into something extraordinary. Marco's creations invite viewers to engage with his work from multiple perspectives, exploring the balance between chaos and order.
